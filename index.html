The year, unbound

twelve months, one surface, many threads

Capture

One Möbius loop carrying both hemispheres: every month is split into an N band and an S band, each shaded for its own season — June light in the north band and dark in the south, December the reverse. Months run twice around the strip, so the spot nearest you always carries two months — one facing you (solid highlight on the month bar) and one on its reverse (dashed highlight) — and both follow live as you turn the loop. A divided loop runs as two half-thickness strands that together equal the whole \u2014 they part at the division, run the hemispheres, and merge back at the reunion. Days carrying scenes or milestones are marked in their storyline's colour (a second ring means several storylines cross that day), and every loop and thread weaves its path through those exact days on the calendars — dipping between the N and S bands when a story crosses hemispheres. The world now hangs in a sky: it turns on its own axis and orbits its sun, the Wanderer, which in turn rides a great year around the elder Far Sun, while a grey Moon — no fire of its own — threads the eye of the loop, chain-linked with the year. One finger drags your gaze · two fingers move you (pinch to fly, slide to drift) · scroll, shift-drag, W/S or the ▲▼ buttons on desktop · tap a month to fly to it · tap a day on a calendar to open it in Display · tap a milestone flag to land on it in Display, where its ✎ edits it.

function doGet(){
  var f=getFile_();
  var out=f?f.getBlob().getDataAsString():'{"empty":true}';
  return ContentService.createTextOutput(out)
    .setMimeType(ContentService.MimeType.JSON);
}
function doPost(e){
  var data=JSON.parse(e.postData.contents);
  var p=data.project||data;
  var body=JSON.stringify({project:p,
    savedAt:new Date().toISOString()});
  var f=getFile_();
  if(f){f.setContent(body);}
  else{DriveApp.createFile('mobius-storyboard-data.json',body);}
  try{updateSheets_(p);}catch(err){}
  return ContentService.createTextOutput('{"ok":true}')
    .setMimeType(ContentService.MimeType.JSON);
}
function getFile_(){
  var it=DriveApp.getFilesByName('mobius-storyboard-data.json');
  return it.hasNext()?it.next():null;
}
function updateSheets_(p){
  var ss=SpreadsheetApp.getActiveSpreadsheet();
  var log=ss.getSheetByName('Backups')||ss.insertSheet('Backups');
  if(log.getLastRow()===0)
    log.appendRow(['Saved at','Threads','Scenes','Notes']);
  log.appendRow([new Date(),(p.threads||[]).length,
    (p.entries||[]).length,(p.notes||[]).length]);
  var sc=ss.getSheetByName('Scenes')||ss.insertSheet('Scenes');
  sc.clear();
  sc.appendRow(['Date','Thread','Character','Title',
    'Location','Archived','Text']);
  var tmap={};
  (p.threads||[]).forEach(function(t){tmap[t.id]=t;});
  (p.entries||[]).forEach(function(en){
    var t=tmap[en.threadId]||{};
    var txt=(en.html||'').replace(/<[^>]+>/g,' ')
      .replace(/\s+/g,' ').trim().slice(0,45000);
    sc.appendRow([en.date,t.name||'',t.character||'',
      en.title,en.location,en.archived?'yes':'',txt]);
  });
  var no=ss.getSheetByName('Notes')||ss.insertSheet('Notes');
  no.clear();
  no.appendRow(['Pinned to','Note','Archived']);
  (p.notes||[]).forEach(function(n){
    no.appendRow([n.type+(n.value?': '+n.value:''),
      n.text,n.archived?'yes':'']);
  });
}

Loops & threads

Loops are the continuous main storylines; threads are the fables and follies that decorate them. Tap a tier or hemisphere tag to change it; ✎ opens the editor.

Cast & objects

People and things that travel between threads. Tap a name to gather their scenes on the workbench.

Milestones

Flags on the loop viewer. Tap ✎ to open one in its storyline’s editor — new ones are planted there too.

The great cycle

Storylines sit on a twelve-hour face — a circle with no start and no finish — and each merges into its neighbour around the ring. Drag a marker to move a storyline; click one to open its editor. Times can also be set precisely in each storyline's ✎ editor.
